Get to Know Us

Nick

Nick is the proud owner of Strictly Biz Barbershop. Hailing from Philly, Nick brings a blend of East Coast style and expertise to our shop. He is passionate about his craft and loves creating a welcoming environment for clients. When he's not at the shop, Nick enjoys spending time with his family, whom he cherishes dearly.

Vu

Vu, originally from Buffalo, is known for his precise cuts and friendly demeanor. A dedicated father of three girls, Vu balances his time between his family and his love for the Buffalo Bills. His attention to detail ensures every client leaves with a smile.

Christian

Christian is a Kansas City fan through and through. With a little girl and two dogs at home, he brings a sense of community and warmth to the shop. Christian’s ability to connect with clients and his skilled hands make him a favorite among our patrons.

Jordan

Jordan, a Denver/New England sports enthusiast, adds a unique flair to Strictly Biz. Married and a lover of comics, video games, and sports, Jordan’s diverse interests translate into creative and stylish cuts. His friendly personality ensures that clients feel at home.
